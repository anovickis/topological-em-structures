## Detailed Notes on Suspect References

### [4] D. Hestenes, "Zitterbewegung in quantum mechanics"

**Cited as:** *J. Math. Phys.* 73, 024017 (2010)
**Actual publication:** *Foundations of Physics* 40, 1-54 (2010). DOI: 10.1007/s10701-009-9360-3

The journal name, volume number, and page number are all incorrect in the paper. The correct journal is *Foundations of Physics* (not *Journal of Mathematical Physics*), the volume is 40 (not 73), and the pages are 1-54 (not 024017). The author, title, and year are correct.

**Recommendation:** Correct the citation to: D. Hestenes, "Zitterbewegung in quantum mechanics," *Found. Phys.* 40, 1-54 (2010).

### [36] A. Novickis, Zenodo (2025)

**Cited as:** A. Novickis, "The Toroidal Electron: A Topological Model of Particle Structure," Zenodo (2025). DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18654710

The DOI could not be resolved via web search or Zenodo search. The Zenodo record number 18654710 is unusually high, suggesting it may not exist yet, may be a draft/private record, or the DOI number may be incorrect. Since this is a self-citation by the paper's author, it may refer to a forthcoming or private upload.

**Recommendation:** Verify the DOI is correct and that the Zenodo record is publicly accessible. If the record is not yet public, consider noting it as "forthcoming" or removing it until published.

### [37] J. Tomsick et al., COSI paper

**Cited as:** *Astropart. Phys.* 168, 102952 (2024). arXiv:2308.12362

The arXiv preprint (2308.12362) is confirmed and real. However, the specific journal publication in *Astroparticle Physics* volume 168, article 102952, could not be confirmed through web searches. The paper appears to have been published in *Proceedings of Science* (PoS), vol. 444 (2024), not in *Astroparticle Physics*. It is possible the journal publication occurred but is not yet widely indexed, or the journal details may be incorrect.

**Recommendation:** Verify the exact journal publication venue. The arXiv link is correct regardless.

### [59] A.F. Vakulenko and L.V. Kapitanski

**Cited as:** *Dokl. Akad. Nauk SSSR* 246, 840-842 (1979)

Multiple secondary sources cite this work with different volume/page numbers. The Faddeev-Niemi (1997) Nature paper cites it as Dokl. Akad. Nauk USSR **248**, 810-814 (1979). The volume 246 vs 248 and pages 840-842 vs 810-814 discrepancy suggests the citation in the dark matter paper may have the wrong volume and page numbers. The paper itself is real and is a foundational reference for the Vakulenko-Kapitansky topological energy bound.

**Recommendation:** Cross-check the exact volume and page numbers against the original Russian publication. The Faddeev-Niemi citation (vol. 248, pp. 810-814) may be more reliable since those authors worked directly in this field.

### [19] G. Bertone, D. Hooper, and J. Silk

**Cited as:** *Phys. Rep.* 405, 279-390 (2005)

This is a minor issue. The paper was received/published online in 2004 and appeared in the 2005 print volume. Some databases list it as 2004, others as 2005. The ADS entry shows 2004PhR...405..279B. The journal details (volume, pages) are correct. The year discrepancy is cosmetic.

**Recommendation:** Optionally change year to 2004 to match the ADS record, but this is not a critical issue.

## Verification Methodology

Each reference was searched using web search engines, targeting:
1. The exact title, authors, and journal information
2. NASA ADS (Astrophysics Data System) for astrophysics papers
3. arXiv for preprints
4. Publisher websites (Springer, APS, Nature, Elsevier, etc.)
5. Google Scholar as fallback

A reference was marked "Verified" if the author(s), title, journal, volume, and year all matched the cited information. "Suspect" was used when the paper exists but one or more citation details (journal name, volume, pages) are incorrect.
